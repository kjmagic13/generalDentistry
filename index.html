We Look Forward to Taking Care of You and Your Smile!

Dr. S.B. Kumar is a General Dentist and has been in practice in Tampa, Florida since 1983. Dr. Kumar graduated from Madras University, India and the New York University Dental Center.

Dr. Kumar is a member of :

  • American Dental Association
  • Florida Dental Association
  • Academy of General Dentistry
  • West Coast Dental Association
  • International Congress on Oral Implantology

Dr. Kumar's coordinated professional team consists of the dentist, dental hygienists, dental assistants, office coordinator and dental laboratory technicians. Dr. Kumar and his team are focused on providing high quality dental care to their patients. Dr. Kumar works with dental hygienists who specifically focus on preventive dentistry and education. In addition, the office and team provide a gentle, safe and caring environment in which patients and their families are afforded the maximum comfort.
